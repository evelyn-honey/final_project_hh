Justin Allsop

Associate

Justin collaborates with an experienced real estate and construction project team helping clients develop specialized contracts for transportation projects and public-private partnerships.

Justin has experience preparing Design Build Operate Maintain agreements and a wide-range of construction contracts for various real estate and government clients developing transportation facilities, large hotels and casinos, sports arenas, industrial manufacturing facilities, and other commercial real estate. He also serves as outside general counsel to a large private company providing design professional, general contracting, and construction management services.
